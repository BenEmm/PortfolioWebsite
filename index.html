What I do

Web Development

I design and develop websites and web applications utilizing technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. My experience includes developing an eCommerce website called "The Game Shop" focused on video game sales, as well as creating a personal portfolio website showcasing my work and personal projects completed during my free time.

Penetration Testing

I offer website and web application penetration testing services using skills acquired from my time pen testing at University. In my professional work I have ran internal phishing simulations with follow up training for small-medium sized organizations. I'm familiar with tools such as Kali, Burp Suite, Nmap, NetCat, OWASP ZAP, Wireshark and Microsoft's Attack Simulation.

IT Support

I specialize in IT support for both on premises architecture and the cloud. I've supported organizations with as little as two people all the way up to 250+ users. I've also supported a wide range of technologies, including Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android. I have a deep familiarity with OneDrive, SharePoint, Windows Server, Windows 10/11 and Azure.
